Privacy Policy

Welcome to NexusCreationStudio! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

Data Sharing

We do not share any of your personal data with third-party companies. Your privacy is important to us, and we do not engage in any data-sharing practices.

Data Collection

We collect only the minimal data necessary for the functioning of our services. This includes:

  • Information you input in login fields: We collect this information to authenticate your identity and provide you with access to our services.
  • Contents of files you create/reupload on our servers: We collect this data to enable the functioning of our services and to provide you with access to your files.
  • Messages you send through our platform: We collect these messages to facilitate communication between users and to ensure the proper functioning of our messaging system. This data is necessary for the operation of our services and cannot be sent without being transmitted to our servers.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. All data transmitted to and from our servers is encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols to protect it from unauthorized access.
